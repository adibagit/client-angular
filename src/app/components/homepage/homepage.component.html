ABOUT US

Solution Ninjas is an application which is a software program that a customer & support team uses to create, manage and maintain a of customer ticket.


• Solution Ninjas is a type of ticketing system to create, manage and report on queries and complaints for a Housing Estate.

• It is a software platform designed to manage and track customer support requests.

• It streamlines the process of resolving customer issues, making it easier for businesses to provide fast and effective support.

• The issue regarding the property log by the customer is called as a ticket.

• Ticket can be then solved by the employees of departments
